What to Expect in Mystical Legends!

Mystical Legends will be an exciting game to play with three gamemodes to choose from. These gamemodes are, Battle Royal, Campaign, and Creative. You will be able to choose from all of these gamemodes at anytime that you want. Read on to see what else will be added.

What to Expect

Below is a list of what you will be able to do in the game.

. Play three gamemodes.

Battle Royal
. Collect items and characters in Battle Royal.
. Save your game. This is done by giving you a save code which you save onto your computer for use when you log back in.
. Collect XP to unlock tiers and gain more rewards.
. Play multiple gamemodes within Battle Royal such as: solo, duos, trios, quads (squads), etc...
. Customize your own first character.
. Customize your locker.
. Collect currency and use them to buy more items and chracters.
. In each match, use various weapons, vehicles, and gadgets to win a victory.
Campaign
. Follow a unique and extensive storyline.
. Interact with other characters and form teams.
. Battle with various weapons and spells.
. Battle and defeat various bosses to claim a special weapon/item.
. Complete the storyline to unlcock exclusive items in Battle Royal.
. Explore various locations and discover new secrets.
. Save your progress.
. Unlock other characters to become them and use their powers.
. Complete quests to unlock more weapons.
. Upgrade weapons, for a price of course, at a weaponsmith.
Creative
. Create your own games.
. Use every weapon that is in Campaign and Battle Royal.
. Place down grounds, traps, obstacles, enemies, vehicles, and more.
. Save one game.
. Edit game rules for your game.
